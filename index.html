The Little Cloud's Big Adventure

In a high sky lived a small cloud, Cotton. He dreamed of exploring.

One morning, Cotton left for adventure, waving to his cloud family.

He played with birds over meadows, laughing joyfully.

At a farm, he and a scarecrow shared a friendly wave.

Over a river, jumping fish played with him. Cotton was delighted.

At sunset, he admired a rainbow, wishing to be colorful.

To his surprise, Cotton turned pink, orange, and purple with the sunset.

Returning home, the stars and moon welcomed him. He shared his tales.

Cotton fell asleep, dreaming of more wonders, ready for tomorrow.
